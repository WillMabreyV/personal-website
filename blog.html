Western is for cutting out tumors, vaccinating against specific viruses (e.g., COVID), repairing organ damage, and addressing immediate physical trauma. Eastern is for chronic illness lacking clear etiology, psychosomatic issues, malaise, stress, and physical/mental unwellness. Western can help address overall wellness and wholistic issues - but does so ,incompletely. Eastern can help prevent specific dieseases like cancer, organ damage, and even viral and bacterial disease - but does so preventatively and indirectly.
